Xbox HDMI Port Replacement

The most common Xbox repair we see. The Xbox Series X is particularly susceptible to HDMI port damage due to its port design. A damaged or bent HDMI port causes no video output, flickering, or "No Signal" on your TV. At our Guildford workshop, we use professional micro-soldering equipment to remove the faulty HDMI port and solder a brand-new replacement — restoring full 4K HDR output. We see customers from Woking, Farnham, Godalming, and across Surrey for Xbox HDMI repairs.

Symptoms:

  • Xbox powers on but no picture on TV
  • Flickering or distorted 4K video output
  • HDMI cable feels loose in the Xbox port
  • Bent or broken pins visible inside port
  • Xbox light on but TV says "No Signal"
