Screen Replacement

The most common iPhone repair. Cracked glass, unresponsive touch, black screen, or green lines — we replace your iPhone screen with a high-quality OLED or LCD panel matched to your model. Every screen is tested for colour accuracy, touch sensitivity, and True Tone compatibility before your phone leaves our workshop.

Symptoms:

  • Cracked or shattered front glass
  • Touch screen unresponsive or ghost touches
  • Black screen but phone vibrates/rings
  • Green or coloured lines across display
  • LCD bleed or dead pixel patches

Battery Replacement

iPhone batteries degrade after 500+ charge cycles. If your battery health is below 80%, you'll notice significantly reduced performance and battery life. We replace batteries with high-quality cells that restore your iPhone to all-day battery life. Most battery swaps are completed in under 30 minutes.

Symptoms:

  • Battery health below 80% in Settings
  • Phone dies at 20-40% battery level
  • Battery drains in under 4 hours
  • Phone feels hot during normal use
  • Swollen battery pushing screen up

£35

Charging Port Repair

A faulty Lightning or USB-C port is a common issue — lint buildup, bent pins, or a worn connector can stop your iPhone from charging. We replace the entire charge port flex cable, which also resolves issues with the microphone, speaker, or haptic feedback on many models.

Symptoms:

  • iPhone won't charge or charges intermittently
  • Cable feels loose in the port
  • Only charges at certain angles
  • Microphone not working on calls
  • Bottom speaker quiet or distorted
Starting From
£45
Book Port Repair

Water Damage Repair

Despite Apple's water resistance claims, liquid damage is still one of the most common iPhone failures. Salt water, chlorine, and even heavy rain can breach the seals. We perform ultrasonic cleaning of the logic board, replace corroded components, and restore full functionality. Time is critical — the sooner you bring it in, the better.

Symptoms:

  • iPhone dead after water exposure
  • Screen flickering or discoloured
  • Speakers muffled or distorted
  • Face ID or cameras not working
  • Erratic behaviour after getting wet
Starting From
£55
Book Water Damage Repair

Back Glass Replacement

Since iPhone 8, all iPhones have glass backs for wireless charging. A cracked back panel looks terrible and can cut your fingers. We remove the shattered glass and fit a new rear panel — restoring both the look and wireless charging functionality of your iPhone.

Symptoms:

  • Cracked or shattered rear glass
  • Spider-web cracks from drop damage
  • Glass fragments coming loose
  • Wireless charging not working
  • Cosmetic damage affecting resale value

Camera Repair

iPhone cameras are incredibly sophisticated — but a drop or impact can crack the lens, damage the sensor, or knock the optical stabilisation out of alignment. We replace front and rear camera modules, fix cracked lens covers, and restore your iPhone's photography capabilities to factory quality.

Symptoms:

  • Blurry or out-of-focus photos
  • Black screen when opening camera app
  • Cracked camera lens cover
  • Front camera / Face ID not working
  • Camera shaking or rattling noise
