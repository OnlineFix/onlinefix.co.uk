Why Choose OnlineFix Over a High Street Repair Chain?

OnlineFix repairs most devices same-day or within 24–48 hours on-site at our Guildford workshop, whereas high street chains such as Currys, CeX and Timpson typically send devices away to a central depot and can take up to 14 days. Every repair is carried out by Tomas, the owner and lead technician, with board-level micro-soldering in-house, a 90-day warranty, and a no-fix-no-fee policy.
