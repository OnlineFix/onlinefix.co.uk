PlayStation HDMI Port Replacement

The most common PlayStation repair we see. A damaged or bent HDMI port on your PS5 or PS4 causes no video output, flickering, or "No Signal" on your TV. At our Guildford workshop, we use professional micro-soldering equipment to remove the faulty HDMI port and solder a brand-new replacement — restoring full 4K HDR output. PlayStation HDMI port replacement is our most requested repair from customers across Woking, Farnham, Godalming, and Surrey.

Symptoms:

  • PlayStation powers on but no picture on TV
  • Flickering or distorted video output
  • HDMI cable feels loose in the port
  • Bent or broken pins visible inside port
  • Console light on but TV says "No Signal"

Blue Light of Death (BLOD)

PlayStation won't turn on, shows a pulsing blue or white light then shuts off? This is commonly called the Blue Light of Death and affects both PS5 and PS4. It's usually caused by a faulty power supply unit (PSU), blown fuse, or damaged capacitors on the APU power delivery circuit. We diagnose the exact cause with professional testing equipment and perform board-level micro-soldering repairs. No fix, no fee.

Symptoms:

  • PlayStation completely dead — no lights, no beep
  • Blue/white light pulses then console shuts off
  • Beeps once then immediately powers down
  • Random shutdowns during gameplay
  • Turns on briefly then goes to standby

Overheating & Deep Clean

Dust buildup and dried thermal paste cause PS5 and PS4 consoles to overheat, throttle performance, and sound like a jet engine. We perform a full teardown, deep clean of all internal components, thermal paste replacement with premium compound, and fan service at our Guildford workshop. This restores your PlayStation to whisper-quiet operation and prevents heat damage to the APU. Same-day cleaning available — serving Guildford, Woking, Godalming & Farnham.

Symptoms:

  • Fan running extremely loud constantly
  • Console shuts down during games from overheating
  • "Console too hot" warning message
  • Performance drops and frame stuttering
  • Hot air blasting from vents
