HDMI Port Replacement

The most common PS5 and Xbox issue. A damaged or bent HDMI port causes no video output, flickering screens, or intermittent signal loss. At our Guildford workshop, we use professional micro-soldering equipment to remove the faulty port and solder a brand-new replacement — restoring full 4K HDR output. We see customers from Woking, Farnham, and across Surrey for this repair.

Symptoms:

  • No picture on TV — black screen
  • Flickering or distorted video output
  • HDMI cable feels loose in the port
  • Bent or broken pins visible inside port
  • Console powers on but TV says "No Signal"

Won't Turn On / Blue Light of Death

A console that won't power on is often caused by a faulty power supply unit (PSU), blown fuse, or damaged capacitors on the motherboard. The PS4 "Blue Light of Death" (BLOD) and PS5 blue/white pulsing light are common variants of this issue. We diagnose the exact root cause — whether it's a simple PSU swap, a blown fuse requiring micro-soldering, or a more complex board-level issue with the APU power delivery. No fix, no fee.

Symptoms:

  • Completely dead — no lights, no beep
  • PS4 Blue Light of Death (BLOD)
  • PS5 blue/white light pulses then dies
  • Beeps once then immediately shuts off
  • Random shutdowns during gameplay

We perform a full teardown, deep clean, thermal paste replacement with premium compound, and fan service at our Guildford workshop. This restores your console to whisper-quiet operation and prevents heat damage to the APU. Same-day console cleaning available — serving Guildford, Woking, Godalming & Farnham.
