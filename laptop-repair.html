Won't Turn On / Charge

A dead laptop is usually a faulty charging port, failed power circuitry, dead battery or motherboard fault. We diagnose the exact cause with proper test equipment and quote before any work starts — no fix, no fee.

Symptoms:

  • No lights, no fan, completely dead
  • Charger plugged in but not charging
  • Turns on then shuts off instantly
  • Charging port loose or wobbly
  • Only works at an exact cable angle

Slow Laptop / SSD Upgrade

If your laptop takes minutes to boot, the fix is almost always an SSD upgrade. We move Windows and all your files across — nothing is lost — and a 5-8 year old laptop boots in seconds again. The best value upgrade there is.

Symptoms:

  • Constant freezing and "not responding"

Battery Replacement

Laptop batteries wear out after a few hundred charge cycles. If yours dies in under an hour, only works on mains power, or the case is bulging (a swollen battery is a safety risk), we fit a quality replacement — usually same-day.

Symptoms:

  • Battery drains in under an hour
  • Only works plugged into the wall
  • Case or trackpad bulging outwards
  • Shuts down at 20-40% remaining
  • "Consider replacing your battery" warning
Quoted By Model
Free Quote

Keyboard & Hinge Repair

Stuck keys, missing keycaps, spill damage or a broken hinge that's cracking the case — we replace laptop keyboards and repair hinge mounts so the lid opens and closes properly again, before the damage spreads to the screen.

Symptoms:

  • Keys stuck, missing or unresponsive
  • Typing the wrong characters
  • Liquid spilled on the keyboard
  • Hinge stiff, loose or cracking the case
  • Touchpad not clicking properly
Quoted By Model
Free Quote
