Logic Board Repair

The logic board is the brain of your MacBook. Failed power ICs, blown capacitors, GPU faults, and short circuits can all cause a dead or malfunctioning machine. We perform board-level micro-soldering to diagnose and replace the specific failed components — saving your MacBook when Apple says it needs a full replacement.

Symptoms:

  • MacBook completely dead — no power, no light
  • Turns on but no display (backlight failure)
  • Random kernel panics and crashes
  • USB-C / Thunderbolt ports not working
  • Fan spinning at full speed constantly

Battery Replacement

MacBook batteries degrade over time — after 500-1000 charge cycles, capacity drops significantly. Swollen batteries are a safety hazard that can warp the trackpad or case. We replace batteries with high-quality cells that restore your MacBook to full-day battery life. Most battery swaps are completed same-day.

Symptoms:

  • "Service Battery" or "Replace Soon" warning
  • Battery drains in under 2 hours
  • Swollen/bulging trackpad or bottom case
  • MacBook only works when plugged in
  • Random shutdowns at 20-40% battery
Starting From
£79
Book Battery Swap

Keyboard Repair

The infamous butterfly keyboard (2016-2019 MacBook Pro) is notorious for stuck and unresponsive keys. Even scissor-switch keyboards on newer models can fail from dust or liquid ingress. We replace faulty keyboards — including full top case swaps when needed — restoring every key to reliable operation.

Symptoms:

  • Keys stuck, mushy, or unresponsive
  • Double-typing or ghost key presses
  • Backlight not working on some keys
  • Spacebar or shift key intermittent
  • Keys physically missing or broken

Water Damage Repair

Spilled coffee, tea, or water on your MacBook? Time is critical. Liquid causes corrosion that spreads across the logic board within hours. We perform ultrasonic cleaning to remove all corrosion, then diagnose and replace any damaged components using micro-soldering. The sooner you bring it in, the better the recovery chances.

Symptoms:

  • MacBook dead after liquid spill
  • Keyboard keys sticking or not working
  • Trackpad clicking on its own
  • Screen flickering or discoloured
  • Charging intermittent or not working

£85
