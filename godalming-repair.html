FAQ for Godalming customers

What postcodes do you cover for Godalming?

All of GU7 and GU8 — Godalming town, Farncombe, Milford, Witley, Brook, Wormley, Hambledon, Hydestile, Hascombe, Bramley, Shamley Green, Wonersh and Chiddingfold. Drop-off is at our Guildford workshop.

Can I cycle or walk in from Farncombe?

Yes — Farncombe station is one stop on the Portsmouth line, and the workshop is a short walk from Guildford station. Several customers come in by bike from Farncombe and Bramley.

How quickly can you turn round a screen replacement?

iPhone screens: usually 60–90 minutes from drop-off if the model is in our regular stock. Because Godalming is so close, most customers drop off, head into Guildford for an hour, and pick up before they head home.

Do you handle Apple's "service required" or activation-locked devices?

We can repair the hardware regardless of the message on screen — Face ID dot-projector faults, "True Tone after non-genuine display" warnings, Touch ID issues, and so on. Activation-locked devices need proof of ownership and we may need you to remove the lock as part of testing the repair.

Are repairs cheaper than going to Apple direct?

Almost always for out-of-warranty devices. Apple quotes a flat-rate replacement; we quote a component-level repair. On older iPhones and MacBooks the saving can be £100–£800.
